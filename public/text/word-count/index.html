Frequently Asked Questions

How is word count calculated?

Words are counted using Unicode-aware segmentation for better multilingual support.

Do empty lines count?

Yes, empty lines are included in the total line count.

Does this tool support emojis?

Yes, emojis and Unicode grapheme clusters are supported.
